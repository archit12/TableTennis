Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College
October 9-10, 2013
The 17th annual Ajay Kumar Garg Memorial Table Tennis Tournament is to be conducted on 9th and 10th November 2013 at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in association with the Ghaziabad District Table Tennis Association. The tournament is held every year in an air-conditioned hall, contains simultaneous matches on various tables. Teams from NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, UP, and Punjab mark their participation in 10 different categories ranging from ‘Under 18’ to ‘Over 40’ for men and women separately in individual and team events. A huge amount is kept as the prize money.
