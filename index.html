Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College
October 9-10, 2013
The 17th annual Ajay Kumar Garg Memorial Table Tennis Tournament is to be conducted on 9th and 10th November 2013 at Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in association with the Ghaziabad District Table Tennis Association. The tournament is held every year in an air-conditioned hall, contains simultaneous matches on various tables. Teams from NCR, Haryana, Rajasthan, Uttarakhand, UP, and Punjab mark their participation in 10 different categories ranging from ‘Under 18’ to ‘Over 40’ for men and women separately in individual and team events. A huge amount is kept as the prize money.

Reviews About The event

“We are highly privileged to have this event conducted in our institution. AKGEC has always been keen to ensure the academic excellence of its students along with providing them an environment to develop themselves as active contributors towards the society, and this tournament has always been a vital platform for us to achieve our objective. The visit of various eminent Table-Tennis players and dignitaries in our college in this tournament is a matter on which we can certainly take pride upon.”
-Dr. R.K. Agarwal Director AKGEC, Ghaziabad

“Every year this tournament brings a new zeal and vigour. Our college organises this event in association with the Ghaziabad District Table Tennis Association with a motive to provide the competent players a platform to showcase their talent and earn honour. We also feel proud to see the organising abilities of our student conveners, coordinators and volunteers.”
-Prof. H.S. Siyan Chief Organiser of the tournament, Dean Hostel, AKGEC, Ghaziabad

“We express a heartiest thanks towards Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College for associating us and organising this prestigious tournament in their institution. We thank Dr. Agarwal, Prof. Siyan for their extreme vital support in conducting this tournament on an annual basis. We also thank players from different states for participating and making this tournament even more memorable.”

-Mr. Praveen Gupta Secretary, Ghaziabad District Table Tennis Association

“We are very happy for the event being organised in our college every year. I thank Director, Shree Praveen Gupta, Siyan sir, and all the students in organising team, it makes me very happy to see the players coming from outside playing with full energy and praising the tournament, I thank all.”

-Mr. Pratap Singh Sports Officer AKGEC, Ghaziabad
